CUET UG 2026 Preparation, CUET Syllabus, How to crack CUET, Best strategy for CUET, Top Universities under CUET

Common University Entrance Test (CUET) ne India ke higher education system ko poori tarah badal diya hai. Ab sirf 12th ke marks ke dum par nahi, balki aapki subject knowledge aur logical ability ke dum par aapko DU, BHU, JNU aur Jamia Millia Islamia jaise top institutes mein admission milta hai. CUET 2026 ko crack karne ke liye aapko ek balanced approach ki zaroorat hai.


  • Understand the Exam Pattern: CUET teen hisson mein banta hota hai: Section IA & IB (Languages), Section II (Domain Specific Subjects), aur Section III (General Test). Har section ki apni ehmiyat hai, isliye pattern ko pehle achhe se samajhein.

  • Domain Subjects Par Pakad: Domain subjects ka syllabus wahi hota hai jo aapne Class 12th NCERT mein padha hai. Agar aapne 12th ki NCERT ko bariki se padha hai, toh aapka 80% kaam wahi ho gaya. Concepts ko deep mein jaakar samjhein.

  • Language Test ki Taiyari: Language paper (jaise English ya Hindi) sirf grammar nahi, balki Reading Comprehension aur Vocabulary ka test hai. Rozana ek editorial padhne ki aadat daalein taaki aapki speed aur samajh dono badhein.

  • General Test (Section III): Isme Quantitative Reasoning, Logical Reasoning, aur Current Affairs aate hain. Rozana 1 ghanta math puzzles aur current affairs ko dene se aap is section mein high score kar sakte hain.

  • Computer Based Test (CBT) Practice: CUET ek online exam hai. Isliye online mock tests dena bahut zaroori hai taaki aap screen par sawal padhne aur click karne ki practice kar sakein.

  • Negative Marking ka Dhyan: CUET mein har galat jawab par marks kat-te hain. Isliye "Tukka" maarne se bachein aur wahi sawal karein jisme aap confident hon.

  • Time Management: Kam waqt mein zyada sawal solve karne ke liye shortcut methods aur calculation tricks seekhein, khaas taur par General Test ke liye.

CUET 2026 sirf ek exam nahi, balki aapke manpasand career ki chabi hai. Agar aap sahi strategy aur NCERT par focus karke padhte hain, toh India ki top university mein seat pakki karna mushkil nahi hai. Hard work ke saath smart work karein aur apne sapno ko sach karein.
