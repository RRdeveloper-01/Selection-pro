RRB NTPC 2026 Preparation, NTPC Syllabus, How to crack RRB NTPC, Railway Exam Strategy, NTPC CBT 1 and CBT 2 Tips.

Indian Railways mein non-technical posts par kaam karna lakho yuvaon ka sapna hota hai. RRB NTPC ek aisi pariksha hai jisme 12th pass aur Graduate dono apply kar sakte hain. Lekin dhyan rahe, isme negative marking aur high cutoff aapke liye chunauti ban sakte hain. Isliye ek sateek strategy ka hona bahut zaroori hai.


  • CBT 1 aur CBT 2 ka Fark Samjhein: Dono ka syllabus lagbhag ek jaisa hai, lekin CBT 2 mein questions ka level aur competition badh jata hai. Shuruat se hi concept ki gehraai mein jaakar padhai karein.

  • General Awareness (Highest Weightage): NTPC mein GA ka section sabse bada hota hai. Current Affairs ke saath-saath Indian History, Geography, aur Static GK (Railways Special) par zyada focus karein.

  • Mathematics (Arithmetic & Advanced): Railway Exams mein Arithmetic (Percentage, Profit & Loss, Ratio) ka dabdaba rehta hai. Formulas aur calculation tricks ko rozana revise karein taaki waqt bache.

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning: Isme aap full marks la sakte hain. Coding-Decoding, Syllogism, aur Blood Relations jaise topics ki itni practice karein ki sawal dekhte hi logic samajh aa jaye.

  • Previous Year Papers (Railway Pattern): RRB apne questions ke pattern ko aksar repeat karta hai. Pichle 10 saalon ke NTPC aur Group D ke papers solve karne se aapko real exam feel milegi.

  • Mock Test aur Accuracy: NTPC mein 90 minutes mein 100/120 sawal karne hote hain. Negative marking se bachne ke liye sirf wahi sawal attempt karein jisme aap 100% sure hon.

  • Railway Specific GK: Railway Budget (ab jo Union Budget ka hissa hai), Indian Railway ka itihas, aur zones ke headquarter se jude sawal zaroor padhein.

RRB NTPC 2026 un logon ke liye hai jo mehnat aur sabr ke saath apne lakshya ki taraf badhte hain. Railway ki naukri na sirf suvidhaayein deti hai balki ek garv ka ehsaas bhi karati hai. Consistent rahein, mock tests dete rahein aur apne sapno ko haqiqat mein badlein.
