AIIMS Delhi Ka Sapna: NEET Mein Top Rank Laane Ka Perfect Roadmap

AIIMS sirf ek medical college nahi, ek brand hai. Pehle AIIMS apna alag entrance exam leta tha, lekin ab NEET ke marks ke basis par hi yahan admission milta hai. AIIMS Delhi ya top peripheral AIIMS (jaise Jodhpur, Rishikesh, Bhopal) mein seat paane ke liye aapko sirf pass nahi hona, balki top merit list mein jagah banani hogi. Iske liye aapki taiyari "Extraordinary" honi chahiye.


  • Target 700+ Marks: AIIMS Delhi ke liye competition itna zyada hai ki aapka target 720 mein se kam se kam 700-710 marks hona chahiye. Iska matlab hai ki har subject mein galti ki gunjayish zero ke barabar hai.

  • NCERT ki "Deep Reading": Sirf NCERT padhna kafi nahi hai, balki uske diagrams ke neeche likhi captions, summary, aur tables ko bhi ghot kar peena hoga. AIIMS level ke questions aksar NCERT ke chhupe hue facts se aate hain.

  • Physics mein Conceptual Mastery: AIIMS level ke logic ko crack karne ke liye Physics mein concepts ki clarity bahut zaroori hai. Numerical solve karte waqt "Why" aur "How" par focus karein taaki ghuma-fira kar puche gaye sawal bhi hal ho sakein.

  • High-Yield Topics Par Focus: Genetics, Molecular Biology, Organic Chemistry ki mechanisms, aur Physics mein Electrodynamics jaise chapters ko itna mazboot karein ki wahan se ek bhi mark na kate.

  • Error Log Maintain Karein: Ek topper aur average student mein yahi farq hota hai ki topper apni purani galtiyon ko kabhi repeat nahi karta. Har mock test ke baad ek "Error Notebook" banayein jisme apni har chhoti se chhoti mistake likhein.

  • Time Pressure Practice: AIIMS ki seat wahi pata hai jo pressure mein panic nahi karta. Har hafte kam se kam 2 full-length tests dein aur koshish karein ki paper 2 ghante 40 minute mein hi khatam ho jaye.

  • Mental Toughness: AIIMS ki taiyari thaka dene wali hoti hai. Rozana meditation ya exercise karein taaki aapka focus bana rahe. Yaad rakhein, AIIMS ka rasta aapke discipline aur self-belief se hokar guzarta hai.

AIIMS ka stethoscope pehenna ek garv ki baat hai. Agar aap aaj apne comfort zone se bahar nikal kar mehnat karne ko taiyar hain, toh agla white coat aapka ho sakta hai. Apne lakshya par nazar rakhein aur har din 1% behtar banne ki koshish karein.
